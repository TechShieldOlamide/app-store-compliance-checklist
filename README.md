# App Store Compliance Checklist

Practical compliance checklists for iOS App Store and Google Play Store submissions — covering privacy, security, KYC/AML, and data protection for fintech and digital lending apps.

Maintained by [Olamide Mohammed](https://www.linkedin.com/in/olamidelatifatm) — Founder, TrustGrid Technology Limited.

## Checklists Available

| Checklist | Description | Platform |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| [iOS App Store Checklist](./ios-app-store-checklist.md) | Full compliance checklist for Apple App Store submission | iOS |
| [Google Play Store Checklist](./google-play-store-checklist.md) | Full compliance checklist for Google Play Store submission | Android |
| [Fintech App Security Checklist](./fintech-app-security-checklist.md) | OWASP Mobile Top 10 security review for fintech apps | iOS & Android |

## How to Use

1. Download the relevant checklist for your platform
2. Work through each item before submitting your app
3. Flag any incomplete items as risks to address before launch
4. Use alongside the [TrustGrid Compliance Templates](https://github.com/TechShieldOlamide/trustgrid-compliance-templates) for full policy documentation
